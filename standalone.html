Web Bluetooth Required

Your browser does not support Web Bluetooth, which is required to communicate with the speaker.

Requirements:

  • Chrome, Edge, or Opera (version 56+)
  • HTTPS connection or localhost
  • Bluetooth enabled on your device

Linux users: You may need to enable chrome://flags/#enable-experimental-web-platform-features for Web Bluetooth support.

Mobile: Chrome on Android works well. iOS requires specialized browsers like Bluefy.
